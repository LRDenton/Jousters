Breakfast & After School Clubs

home

Many children come to club as it provides a safe fun environment for them to enjoy themselves with their friends Whilst their parents are at work, others just because they want to be with their friends.

Before and after school
Our clubs are all based on site in the schools they serve.
Before school parents can drop off at any time. Children can have their breakfast and play with their friends in a calm atmosphere ready for the school day.
At the end of the school day the children can go directly to club for a drink and snack whilst they decide what activity to do.

Activities
Each day different activities are planned for the children, such as: art and craft projects, sports, indoor and outdoor games. And most children will want to join in with these activities. However, they do not have to. Also available are a range of play equipment to enjoy if they prefer, these include: construction toys, board games, quite area, dressing-up art and craft and sport/outdoor play equipment. They are supervised the whole time, free to take-part in both the adult lead activities and their own free-play. We recommend you make a visit and see the club in action anytime! No need to make an appointment, just introduce yourself when you arrive. But feel free to call us first to make an appointment if you prefer.
